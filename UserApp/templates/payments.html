Payment by Cash on Delivery (COD) Order in India:

What is Cash On Delivery?

Cash On Delivery (COD) is a payment option offered to you wherein you order the Book(s) on MyBooks and pay at your doorstep on delivery. The Cash will be collected by our Representative Courier partner delivering the Book(s).
